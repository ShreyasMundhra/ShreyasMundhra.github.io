You can find some of the projects I have worked on below.

  • Car Localization
    • Implemented a particle filter to localize a car for the Self Driving Car Nanodegree on Udacity.
    • Achieved an MAE of 0.112, 0.104, 0.004 for the x-coordinate, y-coordinate and yaw respectively.
  • Image Stitching
    • Implemented an image stitching algorithm in Python using SIFT descriptors, feature matching, RANSAC and homography to stitch five images into a panorama.
    • Used numpy and opencv for image processing and matplotlib for visualization.
  • Sensor Fusion
    • Implemented an Extended Kalman Filter to track a bicycle using LiDAR and RADAR data for the Self Driving Car Nanodegree on Udacity.
    • Achieved RMSE values of 0.0871, 0.0936, 0.4871, 0.4795 for the x and y components of position, and x and y components of velocity respectively.
  • Behavioral Cloning
    • Trained a CNN in Keras to drive a car like a human for the Self Driving Car Nanodegree on Udacity.
    • Used dropout regularization to prevent overfitting.
    • Achieved validation MAE of 0.1 on steering angle predictions from front-facing center camera images.
    • Augmented dataset with left-right flipped images, left and right camera images with a steering angle correction.
  • Traffic Sign Classification
    • Trained a CNN using Tensorflow to detect traffic signs for a project of the Self Driving Car Nanodegree on Udacity.
    • Achieved a validation accuracy of 93% and a test accuracy of 91.9% on the German Traffic Signs dataset.

    • Detecting lane lines in a video for a project of the Self Driving Car Nanodegree on Udacity.
